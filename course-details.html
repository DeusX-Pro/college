Technical Training Programs at Sapele Technical College

3-4 Year Programs 1,387+ Students 8 Specialized Trades
About Our Technical Programs

Since 1955, Sapele Technical College has been training Nigeria's technical workforce with hands-on, practical skills across 8 specialized trades. Located on the serene banks of River Ethiope in Sapele, Delta State, we provide a unique learning environment that combines modern facilities with proven vocational training methods.

Our programs are designed to equip students with the practical skills and technical knowledge needed for immediate employment or self-employment upon graduation. Each trade operates from fully equipped workshops with industry-standard tools and equipment, recently renovated under the Delta State Government.

Motor Mechanic (4 Years)

Comprehensive training in automotive repair, maintenance, and diagnostics. Students learn engine systems, transmission, electrical systems, and modern vehicle technology.

Welding & Fabrication (3 Years)

Master welding techniques, metal fabrication, and structural work. Training covers arc welding, gas welding, metal cutting, and fabrication for construction and manufacturing industries.

Electrical Installation (3 Years)

Learn electrical wiring, installation, and maintenance for residential and commercial applications. Covers electrical theory, circuit design, safety standards, and electrical code compliance.

Building & Block Laying (3 Years)

Construction fundamentals including masonry, block laying, concrete mixing, and blueprint reading. Students gain skills in building structures from foundation to completion.

Furniture & Wood Work (4 Years)

Carpentry, cabinet making, and furniture design training. Includes wood processing, joinery techniques, finishing, and quality craftsmanship for residential and commercial furniture.

Mechanical (3 Years)

Training in mechanical systems, machinery operation, and maintenance. Covers mechanical principles, machine tools, and industrial equipment operation.

Business (3 Years)

Business management, accounting, and entrepreneurship skills. Students learn business operations, financial management, and how to start and manage their own businesses.

Catering & Hospitality (3 Years)

Professional cooking, food preparation, and hospitality management. Training includes culinary arts, nutrition, food safety standards, and hotel management.

Admission Requirements

Sapele Technical College welcomes motivated students who are ready to develop practical technical skills. Our admission process is straightforward and designed to identify students committed to technical excellence.

  • Completed application form
  • Junior Secondary School Certificate (JSS3) or equivalent
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Recent passport photographs
  • Medical fitness certificate
How to Apply

Visit the school office during working hours to collect and submit application forms. Our admissions team is available to answer questions and guide you through the process. Early application is encouraged as spaces are limited.

Program Duration & Structure

Programs run for 3-4 years depending on the trade, with a combination of classroom theory and extensive practical workshop training. Students spend approximately 70% of their time in hands-on training in our fully equipped workshops.

World-Class Facilities

Under the Delta State Government's renovation program led by Governor Ifeanyi Okowa, Sapele Technical College now boasts modern facilities that rival the best technical institutions in the country.

Workshops & Equipment

Each of our 8 trades has its own dedicated, fully equipped workshop with industry-standard tools and equipment. Students train with the same tools they'll use in their careers, ensuring they're job-ready upon graduation.

Campus Infrastructure
  • Modern library with technical resources
  • Seven hostel blocks (capacity for 336 students)
  • 19 senior staff quarters including principal's residence
  • Spacious classrooms with modern teaching aids
  • Medical facility with qualified nurse
  • Sports and recreation facilities
Unique Riverside Location

We are the only technical college in Nigeria built on a riverbank. Our location on the banks of River Ethiope provides a serene, distraction-free learning environment perfect for focused study and skill development.
