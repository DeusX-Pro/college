About Us

70 Years of Excellence in Technical Education

Since 1955, Sapele Technical College has been at the forefront of vocational training in Nigeria. Located on the serene banks of River Ethiope, we provide world-class technical education across 8 specialized trades, equipping over 1,387 students with practical skills for economic independence and nation building.

Industry-Standard Facilities

Each of our 8 trades operates from fully equipped workshops with modern tools and equipment, renovated under the Delta State Government to meet international standards.

Hands-On Training

Our experienced instructors provide practical, skill-based education that prepares students for immediate employment or self-employment upon graduation.

How It Works

Your Journey to Technical Excellence

Apply & Enroll

Submit your application and choose from our 8 specialized trades. Our admissions team will guide you through the enrollment process and requirements.

Learn & Master Skills

Train in our fully equipped workshops with experienced instructors. Gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and techniques over 3-4 years.
